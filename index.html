We Are Keploy

Keploy is a developer-centric backend testing tool. As an open source organisation, we believe everything should be community-driven. Join us on a journey into the domain of technical writing and experience the growth and knowledge that accompanies this opportunity.

The Process

01

Submit Idea

Use our application form to submit your content idea with an outline and a previous writing example.

02

Write the Content

Once your topic is approved, start writing. Additional guidelines will be provided to assist you in the process.

03

Refine

Submissions will be reviewed every 3 months, providing feedback and requesting structural changes for selected topics.

04

Revise

Submit your draft, collaborate with editors, image design, and article prep. Anticipate 1-2 rounds of revisions in 1-2 weeks.

05

Publish

After revisions, we publish your article, send your compensation, and promote it across all our channels.

06

Repeat

There's no limit; you can publish multiple articles. Build a lasting relationship with us by starting the process again from step 1.

Guidelines

Evaluation Criteria

Based on the application, we will review the following:
  1. Originality:

    Submissions should be entirely original, showcasing your unique perspective and insights.

  2. Relevance to Software Developers:

    Content should be tailored for a target audience of beginner to intermediate software developers.

  3. Keyword Volume Requirement:

    Ensure the chosen keyword for the blog has a volume of at least more than 400.

  4. Conformity to Plagiarism:

    Adhere strictly to the plagiarism policy. Avoid any form of reproducing or claiming someone else's work.

  5. Compliance with Terms & Conditions:

    Understand and comply with the terms and conditions of the Keploy Community Writing Program.
