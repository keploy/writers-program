02

Evaluation Criteria

Our comprehensive evaluation process ensures high-quality content that meets industry standards.

Originality

Submissions should be entirely original, showcasing your unique perspective and insights into technical topics.

Relevance to Software Developers

Content should be tailored for beginner to intermediate software developers, addressing real-world challenges and solutions.

Keyword Volume Requirement

Ensure the chosen keyword for your blog has a search volume of at least 400+ to maximize reach and impact.

Conformity to Plagiarism Policy

Adhere strictly to our plagiarism policy. Avoid any form of reproducing or claiming someone else's work as your own.

Compliance with Terms & Conditions

Understand and comply with all terms and conditions of the Keploy Community Writing Program.

Guidelines for Creating Content

Follow our comprehensive guidelines for creating blogs and social media carousels that align with Keploy's quality standards.

Blog Writing Guidelines

  • Minimum 700 words with clear structure
  • Include primary keyword in first paragraph
  • Use H1, H2, H3 hierarchy properly
  • Keep paragraphs under 4 lines
  • Include relevant internal/external links
  • Optimize images (WebP format preferred)

Social Media Carousels

  • Create Instagram carousel for every blog
  • Educational and engaging content
  • Follow brand color scheme
  • Include clear call-to-action
  • Use reusable templates
  • Tools: Canva or Figma preferred

SEO Best Practices

  • Research keywords with 400+ volume
  • Keep URLs under 75 characters
  • Write compelling meta descriptions
  • Use descriptive alt text for images
  • Ensure mobile responsiveness
  • Focus on page load speed

Quality Standards

  • Accurate and up-to-date information
  • Authoritative and credible sources
  • Clear and contextual content
  • Proper grammar and spelling
  • Engaging and educational tone
  • Original and plagiarism-free
